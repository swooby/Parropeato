What it does

Low fuss, quick laughs, proudly ridiculous.

Built for tiny moments of audio nonsense: a family room game, a party trick, a bored-in-line experiment, or a very serious investigation into how wrong one little robot can be.

1

Tap and speak

Open the app, tap to start, then say a phrase, a joke, a name, or a noise with no known spelling.

2

It takes a guess

Speech recognition turns the audio into text. Sometimes brilliant. Sometimes baffled. Always confident.

3

Hear it back

Release and hear what the recognizer decided you said — read aloud on your phone or Wear OS watch.

Privacy

A toy app shouldn't be complicated.

M. Parropeato does not run a developer-controlled server for storing microphone audio, speech transcripts, or text-to-speech content. The app relies on the speech services already available on your device.
